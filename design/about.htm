About

Sub Synch is meant to help you with any subtitles that are not synchronized with your movie. It has happend a lot to me lately to find subtitles that are not correctly synchronized to a movie i wanted to watch. So the solution is pretty simple: instead of looking for a subtitle that will actually be synchronized with the movie, wasting time finding it, getting frustrated if you don't find it like i do now and then, download the first subtitle you come across and just syncronize it to the correct framerate. From my point of view, Sub Synch is pretty straightforward to use, but if you have any problems or you don't understand how to use it, you can use the short informations provided on the subtitles synchronizer page or you can read the faq questions.

Functionality

Sub Synch is designed to synchronize the subtitle in 3 framerates: 23.976, 25.000 and 29.97. Also, you can change the type of subtitle into SubRip (.srt file) and MultiDVD (.sub) files. If you want us to expand the tool, to be capable to synchronize subtitles into more framerates, or to be able to handle more subtitles type, please leave us a feedback on Facebook or Tweeter. Beside the sincronization functionality, using a little help from Google Translator (actually a whole lot of help ;)) you can translate the subtitle in any language you might need.

Future

We haven't planned anything important for the future of Sub Synch but we are wainting for your feedback and requests to see if Sub Synch is being helpful to someone, or it needs some more functionality added. Also, if you encounter any errors or you have a subtitle that the Sub Synch tool hasn't been able to help you with, please let us know.
